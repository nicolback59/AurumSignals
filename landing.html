Strategy Coverage

Five Strategies. Two Markets.

Each strategy is purpose-built for its timeframe and instrument. No one-size-fits-all approach — every setup has defined filters.

MNQ Intraday
5-Minute Chart
MNQ

High-frequency intraday setups capturing short momentum bursts during key Futures sessions. Designed for traders available during market hours with disciplined risk management.

Key Filters
  • HTF trend alignment (1h / 4h)
  • Opening range breakout confirmation
  • Volume profile acceptance filter
  • Session time restriction (RTH / Asian edge)
MNQ Swing
1-Hour Chart
MNQ

Multi-session swing setups targeting 50–200 point moves over hours to days. Built for traders who prefer lower frequency, higher conviction positions with wider targets.

Key Filters
  • Daily structure bias alignment
  • OTE zone (Optimal Trade Entry) scoring
  • Regime filter — excludes choppy sessions
  • Volatility expansion confirmation
MNQ 50-Point
5-Minute Chart
MNQ

Targets clean 50-point intraday moves using momentum confluence and institutional level identification. High selectivity — fewer signals, tighter criteria.

Key Filters
  • Standard deviation band positioning
  • Combo setup requirement (2+ confluences)
  • Pre-market level acceptance
  • Minimum 2:1 reward-to-risk filter
MGC Scalp
3m / 5m Charts
MGC

Gold micro-contract scalp strategy targeting short, high-probability moves during the London and US sessions. Focuses on structure confluences unique to gold's behavior.

Key Filters
  • London session open range playbook
  • Gold-specific RST (Reversal Signal Tool)
  • Correlation filter vs. DXY / Bond yields
  • Intraday liquidity sweep confirmation
How It Works
